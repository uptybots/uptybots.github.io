# FAQ – Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to UptyBots FAQ. Here you will find answers to the most common questions about our uptime monitoring service.

---

## ❓ What is UptyBots?

UptyBots is an online service that monitors website uptime, APIs, domains, and SSL certificates. It alerts you when something goes wrong so you can act immediately.

---

## ❓ How does UptyBots check my website?

We perform multiple checks including:

- HTTP / HTTPS requests
- API endpoints (Basic / Synthetic)
- Ping (ICMP / TCP)
- Port availability

---

## ❓ How often are checks performed?

You can configure monitoring intervals depending on your plan. Typical intervals range from **1 minute to 15 minutes**.

---

## ❓ How do I get notified of downtime?

UptyBots supports multiple notification channels:

- Email
- Telegram
- Webhooks
- On the website / In-app
---

## ❓ Can I monitor multiple websites?

Yes! UptyBots allows monitoring multiple websites, APIs, and services from a single dashboard.

---

## ❓ Does UptyBots track SSL certificates and domain expiration?

Yes, we track SSL certificate expiration dates and domain expiration to prevent unexpected downtime.
